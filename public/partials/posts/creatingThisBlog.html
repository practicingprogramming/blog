The first post is, naturally, about creating this website.

There are a lot of blogging platforms out there, but writing things by myself is the very purpose of this blog. So, I decided to make my own blogging platform from scratch.

I decided to start by making it work first, and then making it better/more generic/whatever maybe later. I also decided not to research how other blogs work but to design it by myself.

So, these are the minimal requirements:

  • Must be able to host static articles
  • Must be able to show those static articles in a feed.
  • Must have "About" page.

Desired but not necessary for launch:

  • Ability to leave comments
  • Convenient publishing of new material

I chose to host in on AWS S3 as it looks like a good option, plus I'm quite familiar with it. Researching totally new technologies is out of scope of this particular project. It is explained in much detail in http://docs.aws.amazon.com/gettingstarted/latest/swh/website-hosting-intro.html
